Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines.
AI systems can perform tasks such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding.
Machine learning is a subset of AI that allows computers to learn from data without being explicitly programmed.
Deep learning is a specialized type of machine learning that uses neural networks with many layers.
AI is applied in various fields like healthcare, finance, transportation, and education.
