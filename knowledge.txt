Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. 
It allows computers to perform tasks that normally require human thinking. 
Machine learning is a subset of AI where systems learn from data instead of being explicitly programmed. 
Deep learning is a specialized form of machine learning that uses neural networks with many layers. 
AI is used in many industries such as healthcare for disease detection, finance for fraud detection, transportation for self-driving cars, and education for personalized learning systems.
